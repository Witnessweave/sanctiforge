The Fire Within — Abel's Story

The Spark

I grew up in a low-income Mexican-American home where nothing was handed out — everything was earned. In 2012 I hit rock bottom. I had lost my job and my car. No savings. No plan. But one thing remained: a fire inside me to create something bigger than my circumstances.

The Flame Takes Shape

I started at the swap meet. Every weekend I rose early, set my table in the hot sun, and hustled. I flipped second-hand gear with a twist: I matched fits with clean sneakers and learned how to build a vibe that people felt more than they saw. By 2015 I launched my first site and began shipping coast to coast. Threads On Fire was born from that grit.

Fire Never Dies

Today Threads On Fire stands for confidence built, not bought. These garments are markers of a journey — the work of hands and the witness of a family. If you feel led, close this scroll with a prayer for the hands that stitch and the hearts they serve.
